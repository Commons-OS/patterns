About Commons OS

Commons OS is building an open-source knowledge graph of organizational patterns to help organizations transition toward more equitable, sustainable, and commons-aligned ways of working.

Each pattern is documented with:

  • 9 standardized sections following the Alexandrian format
  • Commons Alignment Assessment across 7 dimensions
  • Cognitive Era Considerations for AI/automation readiness
  • TypeID (UUID v7) for stable cross-references
  • Bidirectional relationships to related patterns
