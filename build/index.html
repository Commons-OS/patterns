Build Vital Systems

Commons Engineering is not about creating digital paper. It is about bringing vital, resilient systems to life. This is where you can see real-world examples of patterns in action and learn from those who have gone before.

Lighthouses

Lighthouses are organizations, projects, and communities that have successfully implemented Commons Engineering patterns. They serve as beacons for others, illuminating the path to a more vital future.

We are currently conducting desktop research to identify and analyze Lighthouses. This section will soon feature case studies and interviews with these pioneering organizations.
