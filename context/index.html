Understand Your Context

Every living system is unique. Before you can find the right patterns, you must understand the forces at play in your specific situation. This is the first step in the Commons Engineering discipline.

For now, we recommend using the Commons Suit Lite to map your context. It is a simple, powerful tool for understanding your stakeholders, their needs, and the value flows between them.
